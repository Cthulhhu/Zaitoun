Welcome to Zaitoun

Experience the warmth and generosity of Palestinian hospitality through our carefully crafted dishes. At Zaitoun, we honor time-tested family recipes passed down through generations, bringing the authentic tastes of Palestine to your table.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Layla Mahmoud brings her grandmother's cherished recipes to life, combining traditional Palestinian cooking methods with contemporary presentation. Each dish tells a story of our heritage, culture, and the loving care that goes into Palestinian home cooking.
