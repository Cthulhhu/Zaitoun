Our Story

At Zaitoun, we bring the rich culinary heritage of Palestine to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, each dish telling a story of our culture, tradition, and love for authentic flavors.

We take pride in using traditional cooking methods and the finest ingredients, including our signature Palestinian olive oil, fresh herbs, and authentic spices imported directly from Palestine.
